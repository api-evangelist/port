Datadog detects infrastructure problems like latency spikes and error rate breaches, but lacks the organizational context needed for autonomous resolution. Port provides the missing layer by serving as an engineering system of record with team ownership, runbooks, and deployment history. Together, Port and Datadog enable self-healing infrastructure where automated systems can resolve incidents without human intervention.
